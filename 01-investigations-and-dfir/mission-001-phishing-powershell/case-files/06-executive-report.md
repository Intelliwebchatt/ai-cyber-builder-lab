# Executive incident report

**Status:** Draft template  
**Audience:** Business and security leadership

## Executive summary

Write a short, plain-language explanation of what was reported, what evidence shows, what remains unknown, and the recommended decision.

## Business impact

State confirmed and potential impact separately. Do not claim data loss or account compromise without evidence.

## Actions taken

List investigation and containment actions in chronological order.

## Key findings

Summarize the strongest findings and cite event or evidence IDs.

## Recommendations

Prioritize immediate, short-term, and strategic actions.

## Limitations

Explain missing telemetry, synthetic-data constraints, and confidence.

## Personal reflection

Complete in the repository owner's own words.

## AI assistance disclosure

Describe any AI assistance and the independent checks used to validate it.
