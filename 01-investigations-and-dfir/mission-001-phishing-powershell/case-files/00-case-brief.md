## Initial report

At 14:19 UTC on July 15, 2026, a fictional employee reported opening an unexpected invoice attachment. Available telemetry covers mail delivery, process creation, PowerShell logging, one synthetic network connection, failed logons, and the user report.

## Questions to answer

1. What happened, in chronological order?
2. Which observations are suspicious, and which remain ambiguous?
3. What additional evidence is required?
4. What immediate containment would be proportionate?
5. What controls could prevent or detect a similar event?

## Known limitations

- No email headers or attachment are provided.
- No DNS, proxy, firewall, EDR response-action, or memory telemetry is provided.
- The documentation IPs are intentionally non-routable examples.
- Failed logons may or may not be related to the email activity.

The analyst must not infer facts that the dataset cannot support.
